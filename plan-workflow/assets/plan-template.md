## Status

Use exactly one of these states:

- `planned` - The slice is defined but not started.
- `in progress` - Work is underway.
- `ready to verify` - Implementation is complete enough to validate.
- `done` - Acceptance criteria and verification are satisfied.

Keep working the slice until it reaches `done` or a real external blocker is recorded in the completion notes.
